About

Hi, I'm Genatta.
I graduated from Oklahoma Baptist University in 2023 with a BA in Graphic Design and a minor in Computer science. I've been working at Permian Ink since August, 2024, making sticker, heat press, and embroidery products. I have experience with a variety of art and design, including digital painting, vector graphics, photography, screen printing, embroidery, and more. I even coded this website myself from scratch! I'm always looking to learn new things and incoprorate them into my work.
